Why I like Place Vendome:

The band features some of my favorite musiciens, including Micheal Kiske on lead vocals with a impressive almost 4 octave range and clear voice. The guitarist, bass player, and drummer are equally as skilled, allowing the band to make a range of different type of music, from hard rock to melodic softer rock, and from fast to ballad. With each of the 4 albums having a different style and focus, one can always find just the type of rock one is looking for.
